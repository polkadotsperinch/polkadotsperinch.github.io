About

We are a young, agile company that focuses on creating modern mobile and web applications. Our motivation is making you happy, and also millions of your app's users. But we also want us to be happy. During our professional careers we have worked on numerous projects that were half done by someone else. So on one hand you have a not working product, with rotten codebase that only creates unnecessary problems and wastes developer time and your money in the future. On the other hand we have to deal with those problems knowing that it would have been much easier if we just started from scratch. Clearly there was only one thing to be done, and that is us stepping up and making our own little company. We've never been happier, and want the same for you. Our promise to you is full commitment to the project, and delivering the best possible product for your needs.
